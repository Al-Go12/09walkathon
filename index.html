Event Overview

The o9 | Red FM Walkathon is more than an in-person event; it is a key chapter of o9 Walks—o9’s flagship global initiative.

Whether we are walking in the streets of Bengaluru or logging steps virtually from Dallas, Amsterdam, or APAC, every step contributes to making the world a more sustainable place.

  • 10% of your ticket goes to U&I literacy programs.
  • Finish the walk & o9 adds ₹500 to the mission.

FAQs

Is there an age limit?

All ages are welcome. Minors should be accompanied by a guardian.

Is it timed?

The 6K and 9K categories are timed. The 3K is a fun, untimed walk.

Are pets allowed?

Pets are not allowed on the route for safety reasons.

Is parking available?

Limited parking will be available near the venue. Carpooling is encouraged.
